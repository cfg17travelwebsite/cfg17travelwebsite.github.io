    Brussels

    Brussels is too often overlooked as a destination. It has convenient transport and beautiful Gothic architecture, the most obvious example being the Grand Place. See how pretty that is?
    Anyway, that's not even the most important part. Waffles, chips with mayonnaise, chocolate and beer are done to perfection in Brussels. You won't find it better anywhere else, including pesky Switzerland.
