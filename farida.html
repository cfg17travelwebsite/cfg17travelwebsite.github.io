Venice

Forget Disney Land, if you are looking for magic come to Venice! With astounding natural beauty and the most amazing architecture, gondolas and violins playing at midnight Venice is possibly the only place that can simultaneously be so magical and yet feel like home. And um did I mention the food?
Handmade pasta and pizza, freshly tossed salads with artisan bread, balsamic vinegar and olive oil, a million flavours of gelato; what more could you ask for? Italians take their food seriously and every bite is fresh and full of flavour. My new favourite flavour of icecream: Fior di latte. It's so simple to make that with just some milk, cream, sugar and a little bit of cornstarch you can make some to try at home! Use this recipe right here.
